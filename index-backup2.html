// THE SCIENCE

Why this data matters

✈

Contrail-induced cloudiness

Airplane contrails evolve into cirrus clouds that trap heat. Their short-term warming effect may exceed aviation CO₂. Smoggy collects ground-truth data that satellites cannot.

✓

Verified, sellable data

Every observation passes Google Vision AI before entering the dataset. No photos stored. GDPR compliant. Peer-reviewable data points.
